About Us

SHARONDENNIS taxi main goal is the efficient organization of passenger transport via taxis and limousines. It is a booking platform for taxis and limousines, which optimizes the booking process for both,customers and drivers. With nowadays technology and its intelligent adoption to taxi and limousine booking customers and drivers can profit. This Win-Win is created by utilizing methods and technologies from the airline industry and can lead to a better route planning and capacity utilization of taxis and limousines. The driver benefits through higher profits and revenues. The customer profits through discounts.
